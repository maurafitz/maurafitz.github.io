Google Doodle

Halloween 2015 Game

While participating in Google's Engineering Practicum internship during Summer 2015, I worked on the Halloween Google Doodle for that year. The goal is to collect as many candies possible for the colored team that you choose. At the end of the game, the global scores for each team is displayed.

The game was built using HTML5, CSS, and Javascript.
